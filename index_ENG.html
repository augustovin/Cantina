Cantina
di David

Cantina di David is a traditional Italian restaurant since 1997, when we decided to bring authentic Italian food and culture to The Netherlands. The essence of our cuisine emanates from all 20 regions of Italy, from the high Alps to the arid land of Sardinia. We offer you many seasonal dishes, using regional products, carefully selected to bring you the flavours of Italy.
We are located in the heart of Holland, nearby Utrecht central station. We are the ideal meeting point for all occasions. Our close interaction with our customers creates a cosy family environment.

History

Cantina di David started in 1997 as small family business motivated by refusing the fast-pizza-culture as an example of Italian cuisine. We serve the original Italian flavour; So, we created a small Italy in Holland, a venue in town to share and feel home.
As the time has passed, commensals, friends and crew enjoy the traditional way to cook, serve and eat. Cesar, Mauro and Angelo –our cuisine staff– have been cooking their original recipes since the beginning of Cantina. Later Piero Rossi, Gino and the Canatar’s brothers joined our team to provide even more quality with their experience in service and business.
Today we are a proud and strong team that continues to enjoy doing their job: cooking and serving original Italian food to your table.

LOCATION

Menu

The menu card combines a diversity of dishes and techniques from the entire landscape of the peninsula. Taste genuine recipes made with local fresh ingredients in combination with our carefull selection of Italian products. We serve slow food as opposed to fast food. We believe that food deserves attention and time; that’s why all dishes are cooked with dedication. Some dishes need hours or even days of preparation before served.

Wine

Italy offers such a wide variety of wines, that it is possible to have good balanced wine list by only randomly picking Italian wines. Our selection of 30 wines is focused to guarantee an harmony between our seasonal dishes and a variety of grapes coming from the major wine regions in Italy as Piedmont, Tuscany , Sicily and Veneto.

INGREDENTS

The richness of our cuisine lies in its diversity. In such a narrow and long country we are vast with 20 different regions with a lot of cultural influences. As a consequense of this mixture, original cheeses, hams, pastas and wines have been created. Those products have been improved by the time and region. There is no way to replicate those conditions.Therefore Cantina only uses Italian products and local fresh ingridents.
